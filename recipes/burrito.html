Burrito

A Protein rich Burrito without meat

This dish makes your hunger go away and doesn't take much of your time to get cooked.

All you need is a couple of ingredients and a frying pan.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Beans
  • Onions
  • Tofu
  • Sauce of your choise
  • Some Cheese (if you like)

Follow these steps

  1. Heat the pan and put some oil in it
  2. Cut the onions and put them into the pan
  3. Crumble the tofu and give it to the onions
  4. Let it fry till its getting some rost flavors
  5. Add those Beans and let them get hot
  6. Now add the sauce of your choice and stir the whole thing
  7. Reduce the heat and prepare the tortilla
  8. Pour the cooked mass into the tortilla and add some cheese if you like
  9. Roll it in
  10. Done, bon appetit
