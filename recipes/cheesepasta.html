Cheese Pasta

A cheese pasta dish

This is a quick and easy pasta dish with only 3 ingredients!

It already comes with lots of protein, no need to add an additional source

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choise
  • grainy cream cheese
  • cherry tomatoes

Steps to cheese heaven

  1. Cook the noodles as described on its packaging
  2. While cooking the pasta start frying those cherry tomatoes/li>
  3. Once rost flavor starts froming, add the cooked noodles to the tomatoes
  4. add the grainy cream cheese and stir it till it is all creamy
  5. Enjoy!
